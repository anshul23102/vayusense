How it works

Three layers. One honest answer.

Accelerated by NVIDIA

Clean → aggregate → trend → anomaly-detect, over 5.9M OpenAQ readings, done in 0.25s on GPU (cuDF) instead of 9.34s on CPU. Fresh insight, every time you ask.

Reasoned by Google ADK + Gemini

A Data Analyst agent pulls hard numbers with real tools. A Health Advisor agent turns them into a WHO-aware, plain-language verdict. No hallucinated stats.

Built to be used, not admired

A safety score, a human-cost estimate, hotspot stations, and a straight answer to "is it safe today?": not another chart nobody reads.
